Dragon Flower

Sensational in colour, form and size! Get ready for the oohs and aahs to come rolling in when this bloom unfolds! Totally unique and uncommonly spectacular, Dragon Flower bears a rich maroon flower spathe that reaches up to 36" in length. Its unusual beauty is accented by a dramatic ebony pistil that grows the entire length of the bloom. This is a showstopping variety that is sure to get attention.Dracunculus vulgaris
